## How a card reaches `/today` (the selection rule)
`src/app/today/page.tsx` reads `app_usage` (pinned-first, then recency, limit 12) → `chooseApps(...)`
keeps up to **8 apps that have an adapter** → each adapter's `today()` renders only if it has relevant
data. So a card needs **(A) the app in your pinned/recent top-8** *and* **(B) qualifying data**.
Presence adapters (mood/energy/sleep) + water/journal always render once selected.
