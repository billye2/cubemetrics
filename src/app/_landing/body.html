New · the +XP assistant

Just say what you did. It logs the rest.

Talk or type in plain words — "drank two glasses of water, walked 3,000 steps, mark floss done" — and the +XP assistant files each one into the right app. It shows you exactly what it'll save and waits for your okay: nothing is logged until you confirm, and one tap undoes it.
